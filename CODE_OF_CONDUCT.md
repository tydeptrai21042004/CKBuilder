# Community conduct

Project discussion should remain factual, respectful, and useful to CKB developers and learners.

- Report reproducible technical problems rather than attacking contributors.
- Do not publish private information, credentials, wallet secrets, or real student data.
- Clearly distinguish verified behaviour, assumptions, and future ideas.
- Credit external code, documentation, and test fixtures.
- Accept technical disagreement without personal attacks.

Maintainers may remove spam, harassment, deliberate misinformation, leaked secrets, or unrelated market speculation.
